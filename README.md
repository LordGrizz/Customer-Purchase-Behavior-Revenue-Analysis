# 🚴 Customer Purchase Behavior & Revenue Analysis (SQL + Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchase behavior and revenue trends in a bike sales dataset using **SQL and Power BI**.

The goal is to identify key drivers of sales performance, understand customer segments, and uncover actionable insights to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics to understand purchasing behavior
* Identify high-performing products and categories
* Evaluate revenue trends across time and regions
* Support business decisions through data-driven insights

---

## 🧱 Data Model

The dataset was transformed into a **Star Schema**:

* **Fact_Sales** → Revenue, Profit, Order Quantity
* **Date** → Date, Month, Year
* **Customer** → Age Group, Gender
* **Product** → Product, Category, Sub-Category
* **Location** → Country, State

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL Server (SSMS)** → Data transformation & analysis
* **Power BI** → Dashboarding & visualization
* **Excel** → Initial data preparation

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Customer Analysis

* Analyzed customer demographics (age group & gender)
* Identified key segments driving revenue

### 🔹 Product Analysis

* Top-performing products by revenue and profit
* Category-wise revenue contribution
* Product preference across customer segments

### 🔹 Time Analysis

* Monthly revenue and profit trends
* Running revenue analysis
* Best-performing months

### 🔹 Regional Analysis

* Revenue by state and country
* Top-performing regions

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Certain customer segments contribute disproportionately to revenue
* A small number of product categories drive the majority of sales (Pareto effect)
* Regional differences highlight opportunities for targeted marketing
* Revenue trends show clear seasonal patterns

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Enabled identification of high-value customer segments
* Supported targeted marketing and product positioning
* Provided insights to improve sales strategy and revenue growth

---
