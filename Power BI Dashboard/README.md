#  Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

---

## 📊 Project Summary

This project analyzes bike sales data using Power BI to uncover insights related to revenue trends, product performance, customer segments, and geographic distribution.

The dashboard is built using a star schema data model and interactive visuals to enable data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profitability trends
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Understand customer segment behavior
* Evaluate geographic performance
* Detect low-performing products

---

## 🧱 Data Model

* Star Schema Design
* Fact Table: Sales
* Dimension Tables: Date, Customer, Product, Location

---

## 📈 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue: **85M**
* Total Profit: **32.22M**
* Total Cost: **53M**
* Total Orders: **1M**
* YoY Growth: **59%**

---

# 🏠 Dashboard Pages & Insights

---

## 🏠 1. Home Page (Executive Overview)

### 📌 Purpose:

Provides a high-level summary and navigation.

### 🔍 Key Insights:

* Business generated **85M revenue with 32.22M profit (~38% margin)**
* Strong YoY growth of **59%**, indicating rapid business expansion
* **Bikes** is the top-performing category
* **United States** is the highest revenue-generating country
* Revenue shows peak in **June and December**, indicating seasonal demand

---

## 📊 2. Sales Performance Page

### 📌 Purpose:

Analyzes revenue trends and sales drivers.

### 🔍 Key Insights:

* Revenue increased significantly from **2013 to 2015**, followed by slight decline
* **Accessories generate highest order volume**, but not the highest revenue
* Top products:

  * Water Bottle (30 oz)
  * Patch Kit
  * Mountain Tire Tube
* Monthly trend shows dip around **July–August**, followed by recovery
* Sales are concentrated in a few high-performing products

---

## 👥 3. Customer Analysis Page

### 📌 Purpose:

Analyzes customer segments (Age Group, Gender).

### 🔍 Key Insights:

* **Adults (35–64)** are the dominant segment (~highest contribution)
* Followed by **Young Adults (25–34)**
* Seniors contribute very little to sales
* Gender distribution is relatively balanced, with **slight male dominance (~51%)**
* Accessories are popular across all age groups

---

## 📦 4. Product Analysis Page

### 📌 Purpose:

Evaluates product performance and profitability.

### 🔍 Key Insights:

* **Bikes category dominates total revenue**
* Top product: **Road-150 Red**
* Highest profit product: **Mountain-200 Black**
* Sub-category leader: **Road Bikes**
* Several products show **low profit despite sales → optimization opportunity**
* Clear gap between top and bottom-performing products

---

## 🌍 5. Geo Location Analysis Page

### 📌 Purpose:

Analyzes geographic sales distribution.

### 🔍 Key Insights:

* **United States dominates revenue contribution**
* Top state by revenue: **California**
* Highest profit margin state: **Kentucky (~60%)**
* Other key markets: Australia, UK, Germany, France
* Opportunity to improve performance in lower-contributing regions

---

## ⚠️ Challenges & Solutions

* No real customer ID → used **segment-based analysis (Age & Gender)**
* Built a **star schema** for better performance and scalability
* Used **DAX measures** for dynamic KPIs and ranking

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX
* Data Modeling (Star Schema)

---

## 📌 Business Value

This dashboard enables:

* Identification of high-performing products and markets
* Detection of low-profit products for optimization
* Understanding of customer segments
* Better strategic decision-making
